Research

Working Papers

Broadband Expansion and Mental Health Outcomes

Working Paper · 2024–present

This paper examines the causal effect of broadband internet expansion on county-level mental health outcomes in the United States, using FCC Form 477 data and staggered difference-in-differences methods (Callaway–Sant'Anna). Heterogeneity analysis uses causal forest methods.
